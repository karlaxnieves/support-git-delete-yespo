To send mobile push notifications via Firebase Cloud Messaging (**FCM**), register the project in Google Firebase, create a key, and upload it to Yespo.

## Creating a Project

1. Go to the <a rel="nofollow" href="https://firebase.google.com/" target="_blank"> Google Firebase</a> service page and click **Get started**.

<Image align="center" width="80% " src="https://files.readme.io/f66899bd2c6278352de5e7b661ccd1761ca5db31d548b4da14592ae9056fb2fe-fcm-1.webp" />

2. Click the **Create a project** button.

<Image align="center" width="80% " src="https://files.readme.io/9db50654fb17c6adaf55c7957aac679487a825a4a73706d7f772847b5ffd8a19-fcm-2.webp" />

3. Name the project and accept the terms of use; click **Continue**.

<Image align="center" width="80% " src="https://files.readme.io/fcb705951f7d1b1b0d379064e78b2d40e907c999ae4a5c0fc088faf3cc442891-fcm-3.webp" />

4. Activate or deactivate the use of Google Analytics for the project; click **Continue**.

<Image align="center" width="80% " src="https://files.readme.io/1bbf13afb1dcd850b2b56156bca5f87e41e0b84573f23df7b63f1f73911848dc-fcm-4.webp" />

If you have agreed to use Google Analytics, select or create a Google Analytics account; click **Create project**.

<Image align="center" width="80% " src="https://files.readme.io/5e2529b796e6a5d06b3522d57b2853ff6dfb9c745cd9670e045fea4e04d4e5ac-fcm-5.webp" />

5. Your new project is ready; click **Continue**.

<Image align="center" width="80% " src="https://files.readme.io/487b22b632af7cda0fe3e817190a60fb7fda9079f1358c954710e9a0aa384052-fcm-6.webp" />

## Generating a Key

1. In the left panel, click the settings wheel next to **Project Overview** and go to the **Project settings** menu.

<Image align="center" width="80% " src="https://files.readme.io/3172d5f32dfc854d7c9bd8b8e45f4db9757eee59ff3a5bc94a120a44d3bbc578-fcm-7.webp" />

2. Go to the **Service accounts** tab and click the **Generate new private key** button.

<Image align="center" width="80% " src="https://files.readme.io/330f3488fe722614c6f6e78060120f1c61162529d64b7e3c7fabb4893bfed9b2-fcm-8.webp" />

3. Click **Generate key** In the dialog box.

<Image align="center" width="80% " src="https://files.readme.io/401613fdb04d2f43ed8af87ebc6c4b40ace334679c8adc9f5471ceb8e9f05cc5-fcm-9.webp" />

4. Download the key to your device.

## Uploading a Key to Yespo

Go to your Yespo account settings to the **Mob Push** tab, open one of the previously created applications or create a new one and upload the key file.

<Image align="center" width="80% " src="https://files.readme.io/e5516151c7516fdb2db93ed5ecfdfab4e03e5d42dad8f5f7f878751f29b048c9-fcm-10.webp" />

[More on connecting mobile app >](https://docs.yespo.io/docs/connecting-mobile-apps)
