You will be directed to the **Account Activation** page to complete the process.

* **Login data**

Enter your password into the **Password** and **Password Again** fields, and click **Next**.

<Image align="center" width="80% " src="https://files.readme.io/9d1eb5a54d9b24dfc0a90a2bfccd0c1c4b641075e42f7cbad1629d3fae19b5e7-how-to-sign-up-005.webp" />

Your password has to comply with the conditions listed below the fields.

* **Account information**

Enter the preferred name for your account and click the **Next** button.

1. From the dropdown list, select:

* Country
* Audience communication language (default language for [multilingual messages](https://docs.yespo.io/docs/multilanguage-overview#/) and publications)
* Currency
* Business type

2. Activate the checkboxes next to¬†the **Contact base source**.
